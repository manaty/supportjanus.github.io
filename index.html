Researches

The Janus cosmological model is bimetric model of the universe describing the gravitation by a pair of coupled field equations extending the Einstein General relatvity equations.
In this model the two metrics describe two parts of the universe, one holding positive masses (the sheet we live in) and the other holding negative massses.
In those two sheets the physic laws are similar but they differ by the value of the units and the physical constants.
For instance the in the negative masse sheet, speed light is higher and distances are shorter, hence allowing faster travel.

Janus model is a living subject where constant progress is made; it invloves the following domains:

Cosmology

Quantum mechanics
